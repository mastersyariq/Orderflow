OrderFlow & Heatmap Terminal (Prototype)
Version 1.2.0 Developed by Mastersyariq via AI Studio

Overview
This is a professional-grade trading terminal designed for advanced order flow visualization and order book heatmap analysis. 
All real-time data is powered by the Bybit WebSocket API.

How to Use
- Navigation: Your default view is the Heatmap Page. To switch between views, 
use the navigation button located in the header next to the "Pair Selection" menu.

- Pair Selection: You can change the trading pair you wish to analyze via the dropdown menu in the header section.

Current Limitations
- Data Persistence: The historical footprint table is currently incomplete. Data accumulation begins only once the terminal is active, 
please keep the platform open to maintain continuous data tracking.
- Data Source: This terminal exclusively tracks data only from the Bybit API. 
It does not aggregate liquidity or volume from other exchanges or Defi. 
Please factor this into your risk management to avoid liquidation.
